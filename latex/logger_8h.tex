It is used for logging, and is a short hand of the {\ttfamily \doxylink{classLogger_a2a6257127d551b26ba54edb9ddfe31f0}{Logger\+::log()}}. 


\begin{DoxyParams}{Parameters}
{\em priority} & is the {\ttfamily Log\+Priority} of the log. \\
\hline
{\em message} & is the log message.\\
\hline
\end{DoxyParams}
It is used as an alternative of {\ttfamily \doxylink{classLogger_a2a6257127d551b26ba54edb9ddfe31f0}{Logger\+::log()}}~\newline
Main objective is, to automatically pass static arguments like {\bfseries{thread id}}, {\bfseries{line number}} and {\bfseries{function name}} into {\ttfamily \doxylink{classLogger_a2a6257127d551b26ba54edb9ddfe31f0}{Logger\+::log()}}.~\newline
This macro internally calls the {\ttfamily log()} with all the necessary arguments passing by it self.~\newline
So user have to pass only necessary parameters.
